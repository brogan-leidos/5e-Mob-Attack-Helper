Mobby - A DnD 5e Mob Attack Helper

About Mobby 5e
Hello, and thank you for checking out my site!
I started development on Mobby5e after realizing that my necromancer PC was about to break into a new realm of action economy that would likely make me very happy, and other plays at the table very unhappy.
What started as a simple script for a necromancer, rapidly evolved into a general tool that could be used by any class, and even DMs!
Fast forward a few months of work, and here we are at the official release of Mobby5e. I couldn't be more excited to share it with you all. Mobby5e is protected unter the MIT liscense, please feel free to find more information on that, and to see the source code at it's Github page.
