How to use:
1. Be sure to set the Target AC in the first box
2. Create a "Mob", either from a pre-set, or customize your own. Make as many as suite your needs.
3. Clicking the Launch Attack button will cause all active mobs to make a single attack against the target!
(Discovery mode active)
